About Me

My name is Kris Aldrich. I was born in St. Petersburg, Florida but have spent most of my childhood growing up in Defiance, Ohio. I went to Miami University out of high school, where I got a bachelor's degree in Zoology and a master's degree in Environmental Science. After several years, I realized that academic work wasn't for me and I needed a change. I am interested in computer engineering and networking and I am currently pursuing several degrees from Northwest State Community College. I've lived and traveled all over the United States, from Baton Rouge, Louisiana to Portland, Oregon and everywhere in between.
